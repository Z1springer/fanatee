Manatees Are Great

The Sea-Cow, or Manatee is a widely known aquatic mammal, best known for their size and calm demenore.

Fun-Facts

  • Due to their shape and size they are thought to have given rise to the legend of Mermaids
  • These cudly looking cretures usually remain in the water, but come to the surface for air about every 5 minutes
  • Strangly enough, Manatees are more closely related to Elephants than they are to other marine life
  • You may notice that they usually do not move their head so much, this is because their heads cannot turn on an axis like ours
